What is PBL?

At DRSS the curriculum relies heavily on PBL which stands for Project Based Learning. Project Based Learning is a method where students gain knowledge and skills by working for a period of time to investigate a question, problem, or challenge. Throughout the year we have done many projects to help us better understand our knowledge for the subjects we are taught, for example during the plate tectonics unit we made models to demonstrate how the earth moved. I think PBL is important because some students benefit from physically touching or discovering what they are learning about.
