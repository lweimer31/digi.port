Genius Hour

This year I worked on the genius hour project. Genius hour is basically when each student can research any topic that interests them, they later have to write a report on their topic and apply what they learned in their project. I worked on this project in my STEM foundations class, so everything I worked on had to be put into a webpage. I think I grew the most during this project, I learned how to apply my knowledge to a webpage and keep it looking professional. Also I learned new things in CSS (Casscading Style Sheets) such as a dropdown navigation bar. Overall I enjoyed this project, it was stressful at times but in the end it was very rewarding.

These images show my codding progress ass I learned a little more about CSS and styling throughout the project. As you can see my coding slowly gets better and better, my favorite page is the converse page because of the gradient background. I the first image something that really stands out it me is how I didn’t even have a proper navigation bar. I learned of this in one year so I am very proud of myself.
