Introduction

Welcome to my digital portfolio! My name is Letty Weimer and I am a graduating senior at the Dayton Regional STEM School. I enrolled at DRSS in eighth grade and since, have enjoyed the education I received. My favorite subjects have been Anatomy and Physiology, Economics, and Current World Issues. Next year, I will attend Colorado Christian University to get my Bachelor of Science in Nursing. My goal is to become a travelling nurse and offer humanitarian aid abroad.

This website displays my projects and accomplishments since middle school. I currently participate in many school clubs and activities, which you can read about on my “Experiences” page.
